Workshop Speakers

Faiza Rico

Faiza is a multimedia artist and graduated BA Broadcast Journalism as Magna Cum Laude. She will share her knowledge about “Typography”.

Chrysthelyn Siena Quides

Siena is a Illustrator, Graphic Artist, Video Editor and a Photographer. She will talk about “Photo Manipulation”.

William Añonuevo III

William is a Web Designer and Brand Specialist in Greymouse. He will be talking about “Web Design 101: The Nuts and Bolts of Building your Online Powerhouse”.

Karl Vasquez

Karl is a freelance graphic artist and he will be sharing how to create “Vector Graphics”.

Christian Binamira

Christian is a freelance graphic designer and he will talk about “Lay-out Design”.

Carlo Berjuega

Carlo studied Digital Illustration and Animation in Ateneo de Naga Univeristy. He is a Graphic Artist at Oragon Tri-Media Production. He will talk about “Design Guide for Print and on-screen Media”.

Carl Andre Bongalos

Carl is a freelance web and graphic artist. He will talk about “Basics of Photoshop”.

About Us

Bicol Information Technology Organization is a community of IT professionals who are from and are in the Bicol Region, Philippines. Our primary objective is to share our expertise, conduct regular meetups in Legazpi City, Metro Manila and hopefully soon in Naga City and the rest of the region.

We conduct regular technology talks for free for students and professionals to augment the training needs and expose others to the latest trends in information technology.
