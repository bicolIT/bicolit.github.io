Speakers

Karen Hobi - Wong

TOPIC: VA 101: Working and Earning as a Virtual Assistant

Karen is a start up founder of WoRK Solutions Outsourcing and KWCo Virtual Business Services offering Virtual Assistant Services and Recruitment Services/ VA matchmaking services for potential VA client work and VV to online entrepreneurs based in the US, UK and Australia. She is a graduate of Aquinas University of Legazpi, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Entrepreneurship and currently taking up Master’s in Business Administration, specializing in enterprise development and management at the Divine College of Legazpi. WoRK Solutions and KWCo is currently the main thesis topic developing to a social entrepreneurship program for the master’s degree.

Kristel Joy Codorniz

TOPIC: Video Animation for Business

Kitel is a Creative and Technical Director at Nocturlabs. A former General Manager of Spoon7 Software Ltd. Co. (now defunct) - Legazpi City. She is a graduate of Divine College of Legazpi, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science. Kitel is a professorial lecturer at STI Naga and Aquinas University. A passionate artist and designer with almost 8 years of experience in Graphic Designing.

Julie Anne M. Astor

TOPIC: “Coding or Copying": Virtues of Programmer/ming.”

Julie is a Head Senior Software Engineer at Nexas America. She is a graduate of Bicol University, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science. She’s a professorial lecturer at Bicol College – Computer Studies. Julie specializes in Windows desktop and client-server applications with database and device integration.

Eden Divinaflor

TOPIC: “IT in Banks & Working in Multicultural Environment”

Eden currently working as a Business Analyst and Programmer at Barclays Bank. Barclays is a British Bank with IT resources based in Singapore. She has experience of more than 10 years in the field of IT in Banking as an IT specialist and software consultant who handled Loans, Deposits and Credit Cards Collections systems of BPI and Citibank Singapore. She is also a member of British Chamber of Commerce Singapore

About Us

Bicol Information Technology Organization is a community of IT professionals who are from and are in the Bicol Region, Philippines. Our primary objective is to share our expertise, conduct regular meetups in Legazpi City, Metro Manila and hopefully soon in Naga City and the rest of the region.

We conduct regular technology talks for free for students and professionals to augment the training needs and expose others to the latest trends in information technology.
