Speakers

Vanesa Orbon-Osiana

Van is a Software Quality Assurance Manager and Tester at ASTI-DOST. She graduated BS Computer Science in Bicol University and currently taking a Master’s Degree in Technology Management at University of the Philippines. She will talk about “Intro to Software Quality Assurance”.

Mark Heddison Bacsain

Mark is a graduate of Computer Science from STI Legazpi and has more than 8 years of experience as an IT professional working with Microsoft technologies and banking software. He will talk about “Business Intelligence and Crystal Reporting using MSSQL”.

Magno Conag III

Magno is a homegrown Technology Entrepreneur based in Naga City. Having founded a startup in 2011, he will share the experience in “How Nueca Grew from Being a Startup to a Real Business”.

About Us

Bicol Information Technology Organization is a community of IT professionals who are from and are in the Bicol Region, Philippines. Our primary objective is to share our expertise, conduct regular meetups in Legazpi City, Metro Manila and hopefully soon in Naga City and the rest of the region.

We conduct regular technology talks for free for students and professionals to augment the training needs and expose others to the latest trends in information technology.
